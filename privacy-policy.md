# 🔤 Riddles Words — Privacy Policy
**Last updated: June 17, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Riddles Words** (`com.devendrap7.riddles.words`), a wordplay, anagram, and word riddle puzzle game developed by Devendra Prajapati. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the app, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

By using Riddles Words, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. What the App Does

Riddles Words presents 100 wordplay, anagram, and language riddles of ascending difficulty. You read each puzzle and type your word answer. Hints are unlocked by watching rewarded ads; the full solution is revealed by watching one additional rewarded ad. All puzzle progress is saved locally on your device — no account or internet connection is required to play.

---

## 3. Information We Collect

We do **not** collect any personally identifiable information directly. However, the third-party services we use may collect the following automatically:

- **Device & usage data** – device model, OS version, app version, session duration, and feature interactions (via Firebase Analytics).
- **Crash reports** – stack traces and device state at the time of a crash (via Firebase Crashlytics). No personally identifiable content is included.
- **Advertising identifiers** – your device's advertising ID (GAID) and IP address, used by Google AdMob to serve relevant ads and apply GDPR consent signals.

**Game data** (level progress, settings, onboarding status) is stored *exclusively on your device* using Hive local storage. It is never uploaded to any server. The app does not read, store, or transmit any text you type.

---

## 4. How We Use the Information

- Analyse which word puzzles players find most engaging so we can improve content quality.
- Detect and fix crashes to maintain app stability.
- Serve advertisements (rewarded, banner, interstitial, and app-open) to keep the app completely free.
- Comply with legal obligations, including GDPR consent management.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

Riddles Words integrates the following third-party SDKs. Each has its own privacy policy:

- **Google AdMob** – displays ads and manages ad revenue. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Firebase Analytics** – app usage analytics. [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **Firebase Crashlytics** – crash reporting. [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

We do **not** use Firestore, any cloud database, login services, or social SDKs.

---

## 6. Advertising & Consent (GDPR / CCPA)

On first launch, the app displays a consent form (Google UMP / Funding Choices) as required by GDPR and similar regulations. You may choose to:

- Consent to personalised ads (based on your interests).
- Opt for non-personalised ads (contextual only).

You can change your consent choice at any time via *Settings → Privacy* inside the app. The app is completely free; ads are the only monetisation method — there are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or premium tiers.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Riddles Words is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through one of our third-party services, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

---

## 8. Data Retention & Deletion

All game data lives on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app. Analytics and crash data retained by Google/Firebase follows [Firebase's own retention policies](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy). We do not maintain any independent copy of user data on our servers.

---

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including access, correction, deletion, and objection to processing of your personal data. For data held by Google (Analytics, AdMob, Crashlytics) you may submit requests via [Google Account settings](https://myaccount.google.com/). For any other queries, contact us directly.
